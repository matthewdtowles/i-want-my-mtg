We are starting a blog. This is where we will write about the things that do
not fit on a feature page: how to think about collection value, what changed
in a release, and the occasional deep dive into Magic finance.

## What to expect

A few kinds of posts will show up here over time:

- **Guides** that go further than the in-app help, like how to read your
  portfolio's profit and loss or when a price alert is worth setting.
- **Product updates** when something meaningful ships.
- **Market notes** on what is moving and why, written for collectors rather
  than speculators.

## Why a blog at all

Tracking a collection is partly a tooling problem and partly a knowledge
problem. The app handles the tooling. The blog is for the knowledge: the
habits and judgment calls that make the numbers mean something.

If there is a topic you want covered, the GitHub repository linked in the
footer is the best place to ask.
